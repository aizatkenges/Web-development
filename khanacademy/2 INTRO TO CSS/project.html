Travel to the most beautiful countries

South Korea

South Korea is a dynamic mix of high-tech cities and traditional culture. In Seoul, visit Gyeongbokgung Palace and enjoy shopping in Myeongdong. Explore Jeju Island for natural wonders like Hallasan Mountain and stunning beaches. Don’t forget Busan for its coastal vibe, and dive into Korean cuisine by trying bibimbap, Korean BBQ, and street food.

What you should do?
  1. Stroll through Bukchon Hanok Village.
  2. Visit Namsan Tower for stunning views of Seoul.
  3. Explore the DMZ (Demilitarized Zone) for historical insight.
  4. Try beauty shopping and street food in Myeongdong.

Japan

Japan offers a unique blend of modern innovation and ancient tradition. In Tokyo, explore bustling districts like Shibuya and Shinjuku , and experience the vibrant pop culture. Don’t miss Kyoto’s historic temples, such as Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), or the stunning cherry blossoms in spring. For relaxation, soak in an onsen (hot spring) and enjoy traditional Japanese cuisine like sushi and ramen.

What you should do?
  1. Visit Mount Fuji and Hakone.
  2. Experience a tea ceremony in Kyoto.
  3. Explore Tokyo Disneyland or DisneySea.
  4. Wander through Osaka’s Dotonbori district for street food.
