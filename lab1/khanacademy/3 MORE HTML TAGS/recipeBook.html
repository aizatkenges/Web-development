Classic Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 1 cup
Baking powder 2 tsp
Sugar 2 tbsp
Milk 1 cup

Step 1: In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.

Step 2: In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, butter, and vanilla. Combine with dry ingredients.

Step 3: Heat a non-stick pan, pour batter, and cook until bubbles form. Flip and cook the other side.
